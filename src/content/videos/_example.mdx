## What you'll see on the watch page

- A responsive player (YouTube embed in this example).
- Poster art driving OG/Twitter images; thumbnails available for UI.
- Chapters in a sidebar; clicking a chapter seeks in supported players or links with `?t=`.
- Captions (VTT) for accessibility and SEO.
- VideoObject JSON-LD emitted via `BaseLayout` from the frontmatter above.
